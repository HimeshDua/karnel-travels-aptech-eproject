Karnel Travel Guide

A Comprehensive Travel Information System

Built with ASP.NET Core MVC

Introduction

Karnel Travel Guide is a web-based information system designed to provide tourists with detailed insights about destinations, accommodations, and transportation.

  • Centralized Platform: One-stop shop for travel info.
  • No Booking Required: Purely informational focus.
  • User-Friendly: Simple, clean, and responsive.

Problem Statement

Travelers often face:

  1. Fragmented Information: Data scattered across sites.
  2. Comparison Issues: Hard to compare prices.
  3. Accessibility: Lack of centralized search.

The Solution

  • Centralizing Data: All entities in one DB.
  • Intuitive Search: Filter by Price, Location, Quality.
  • Categorization: Clear separation of services.
  • Transparency: Availability and capacity display.
